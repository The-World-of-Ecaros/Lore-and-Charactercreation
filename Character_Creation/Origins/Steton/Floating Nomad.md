Floating Nomad
==============

The southern tip of Steton is known to be an untamed wilderness, even to the wild peoples of Steton. The Floating Peaks located their, are home to the more nomadic clans of Steton. These peoples are fond of travel and will often leave their floating homes. But, make no mistake they love their homes fiercely and will always return.  You hail from this area of floating peaks and constant danger. You have thrived with the wildlife and even inherited a natural affinity for magnetism due to your exposure to the forces that cause the mountains to float.  **You manipulate the metallic properties of ammunition, you deal +2 damage with projectile weapons and receive +2 AC against a ranged attack of the same material.

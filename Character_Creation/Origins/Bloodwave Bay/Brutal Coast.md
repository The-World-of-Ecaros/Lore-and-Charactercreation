Brutal Coast
============

Baymen often have their favorite hunting grounds and are often shaped by their prey. Those that hunt the Brutal Coast of Steton, are shaped a little harder than most. The Brutal Coast is not only home to the savage Stetonians but, also to all manners of terrifying creatures. Baymen that face the Brutal Coast come back forever changed by it. They more than their brethren knows what truly lies beneath the water.  You have also been shaped by the Brutal Coast.
